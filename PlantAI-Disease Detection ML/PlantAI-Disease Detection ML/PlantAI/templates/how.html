How Plant AI Works

Plant AI is an advanced system designed to detect diseases in plants using artificial intelligence technology. Here's how it works:

Image Capture

Plant AI accepts images of plants affected by diseases. Users can capture images of plant leaves, stems, or fruits using their smartphones or cameras.

Image Processing

The captured images are processed by the Plant AI system. Advanced image processing techniques are applied to extract features and patterns indicative of plant diseases.

AI Diagnosis

After processing the images, the Plant AI system employs artificial intelligence algorithms to analyze the extracted features. It compares the features with a database of known plant diseases to identify the specific disease affecting the plant.

Result Presentation

Once the diagnosis is complete, Plant AI presents the results to the user. It provides detailed information about the identified disease, including its symptoms, causes, and recommended treatments.
